How FracShr Works

Our innovative platform makes real estate investment simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

1. Property Selection

Our experts carefully select premium real estate properties in prime UAE locations with high growth potential and strong rental demand.

2. Property Upgrade

We acquire the property and implement strategic upgrades to maximize its value and rental income potential.

3. Token Creation

We create digital tokens representing fractional ownership of the property. Unlike blockchain tokens, our proprietary system offers better regulatory compliance and transparency.

4. Token Distribution

Tokens are offered to investors through our secure platform. You can purchase as many tokens as you want, making real estate investment accessible at any budget.

5. Rental Income

We professionally manage the property, ensuring maximum occupancy and rental income. Returns are distributed monthly to token holders.

6. Value Appreciation

Benefit from potential property value appreciation. After a holding period, the property may be sold, with proceeds distributed to token holders.
