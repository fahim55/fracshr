How FracShr Works

Unlike blockchain-based tokenization platforms, FracShr maintains full control and transparency over the tokenization process. We purchase premium properties, upgrade them to maximize value, and create digital tokens that represent fractional ownership. These tokens are exclusively distributed on our platform, allowing us to ensure compliance, security, and seamless profit distribution.

1

Property Acquisition

Our team identifies and acquires high-potential real estate properties in prime UAE locations, ensuring quality and rental potential.

2

Value Enhancement

We upgrade and optimize each property to maximize its rental income potential and long-term value appreciation.

3

Token Creation

We create digital tokens representing fractional ownership of the property. These tokens are issued on our secure platform (not blockchain).

4

Token Distribution

Investors purchase tokens through our platform, becoming fractional owners of the property with proportional rights to rental income.

5

Rental & Profit Sharing

We manage the property and distribute rental income monthly to token holders, minus a small management fee.
