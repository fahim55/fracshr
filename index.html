How It Works

1. Property Acquisition

Our experts identify and acquire high-potential real estate properties in prime UAE locations.

2. Value Enhancement

We upgrade and optimize the property to maximize its rental income potential and overall value.

3. Tokenization

The property is divided into digital tokens (not blockchain-based) that represent fractional ownership.

4. Investor Distribution

Tokens are offered to investors on our platform, with FracShr retaining property rights.

5. Profit Sharing

Rental income is distributed monthly to token holders based on their ownership percentage.
